Chapter 4: The Surnbank Architecture and Governance

The Surnbank Concept

This chapter introduces the Surnbank, a revolutionary financial institution designed to embody Surnumerism’s labor-based principles. Unlike traditional banks, the Surnbank prioritizes the valuation of work, supporting over 2 billion global workers (ILO, 2025) through innovative credit systems.

Architecture and Operations

The Surnbank’s architecture is built on decentralized governance, ensuring that labor contributions dictate financial policies. This section outlines its operational framework, promoting equity in resource distribution.

Governance and Accountability

Governance within the Surnbank involves community representation, aligning with Surnumerism’s call for shared development. This chapter explores mechanisms to maintain transparency and accountability on a global scale, such as community voting systems.
