Chapter 2: Implementing Surnumerism in a Global Context

Practical Application of Labor Value

This chapter delves into the practical implementation of Surnumerism, focusing on how labor can be the cornerstone of economic systems worldwide. By valuing work over capital, communities can develop sustainable models that benefit all, reducing the 2 billion workers affected by exploitative wages (ILO, 2025).

Global Economic Transformation

Surnumerism proposes a shift from traditional ownership to a shared economy. This section explores case studies—hypothetical for now—where nations prioritize labor contributions, fostering innovation and equity across borders.

Challenges and Opportunities

Implementing Surnumerism faces resistance from entrenched economic powers, yet it offers opportunities for a balanced future. This chapter outlines strategies to overcome obstacles, encouraging a global dialogue on work-based prosperity.
