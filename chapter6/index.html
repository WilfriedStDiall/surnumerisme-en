Chapter 6: Countermeasures to Risks – Supply-Demand Balance and Training

Introduction: Anticipating Imbalances

Surnumerism manages risks (overproduction, elite capture) by using currency as a tool to support human labor. Additionally, commodity volatility poses a specific risk in Africa, particularly in oil-producing regions.

Solutions

  • Predictive AI: Avoids oversupply by adjusting investments.
  • Universal training: 20% of funds finance education (e.g., 500,000 AI learners), including an AI training program in Kenya.
  • Transparent Audits: Blockchain for traceability, strengthening monetary confidence.

Example: China (2035)

China's SurnBank finances robot factories, balanced by massive training programs, using currency as cash. In Nigeria, a supply-demand balance is maintained for oil production, mitigating volatility.

Conclusion

Countermeasures ensure stability, anchored in currency. The following chapter explores a national application pilot in Africa.
