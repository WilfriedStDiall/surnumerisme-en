Chapter 9: Challenges and Criticisms – Responses to Skeptics

Introduction: Anticipating Obstacles

Criticisms: complexity, resistance from elites, crypto volatility. Currency, as a medium of exchange, guides the responses. A specific concern is resistance from African elites due to entrenched economic interests.

Answers

  • Complexity: Simplified by AI and blockchain.
  • Resistance: Incentives (labels, exemptions).
  • Volatility: Reserve funds, anchored in monetary confidence. For example, Nigeria mitigated crypto volatility in 2025 with a 10% reserve fund.

Conclusion

The challenges are surmountable, using currency as leverage. In Kenya, Surnumerism countered elite resistance with a 2025 pilot, proving its adaptability.
