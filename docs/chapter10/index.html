Chapter 10: A Universal Call – Publish in Open Source for Humanity

Introduction: Share the Vision

Surnumerism, built on currency as money, is an open-source common good. We call upon the African community, especially thought leaders, to embrace and contribute to this vision.

Strategy

  • GitHub repository for formulas and models.
  • Partnerships with universities and NGOs, moralizing currency, including collaborations with African universities such as Lagos and Dakar.

Conclusion

  • Muhammad speaks of the Book of God as a Reminder... to humanity.
  • Wilfried St Dial thinks of Surnumerism as a Call... to humanity, with a special invitation to Africa to lead this open-source movement.
