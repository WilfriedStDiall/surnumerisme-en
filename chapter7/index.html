Chapter 7: National Implementation – Pilots in Europe and Asia

Introduction: Local Tests

National pilots validate the effectiveness of Surnumerism, using currency as a lever for equity. A pilot is also being prepared in Senegal to test the model in a developing economy.

Case Study: France

Integration with the CSG (Contribution Sociale Généralisée), financing green startups (+1% GDP), via monetary confidence. Companies benefit from a tax exemption on reinvested CapitalSurn.

Case: China

Harmonization with the Belt and Road Initiative, creating 2 million jobs, moralizing the currency. A parallel pilot with Nigeria (China-Nigeria partnership) finances solar infrastructure.

Conclusion

Pilot projects prove feasibility, anchored in currency as legal tender. The next chapter scales Surnumerism globally, starting with a pan-African pilot in Senegal and Nigeria.
